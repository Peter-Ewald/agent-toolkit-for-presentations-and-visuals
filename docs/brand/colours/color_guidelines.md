## COLOUR RATIOS
The colours are divided into three groups. 

Striking the right balance in the use of colour is essential to keep a strong brand recognition. It is a core principle to always have enough of our two primary colours; cyan and white to ensure this. 

The secondary colours can be used for texts, backgrounds and illustrations - always together with the primary colours. 

The spot colours are only used for calls to actions in marketing materials, and in very small doses.

## Body Texts

When writing body text, using different sizes can create a clear hierarchy, improve readability, and guide the reader’s eye so essential information stands out while supporting details remain unobtrusive. You can find below 3 different sizes of body text.
Large body text

For introductory paragraphs, key messages, or content that needs emphasis.

NunitoCustom Light
Size: 22 pt
Line spacing: 25,5 pt
Regular body text

Default size for most content. It ensures comfortable reading and maintains consistency throughout longer text blocks.

NunitoCustom Light
Size: 18 pt
Line spacing: 22 pt
Small body text

For secondary information such as captions, disclaimers, or footnotes.

NunitoCustom Regular
Size: 14 pt
Leading: 18 pt
