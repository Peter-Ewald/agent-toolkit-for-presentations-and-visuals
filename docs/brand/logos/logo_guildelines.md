# Trademark

OUR LOGO IS OUR TRADEMARK


It is only to be used in its pure form and on official Ramboll material. 
Our logo or elements from it must not be used to create alternative logos or sub-logos. 

When standing alone or on a light backgrounds, our logo is presented using the Ramboll cyan colour. On darker backgrounds – and for visibility purposes – it is coloured in white. 

When placed on top of an image, the image shows through the logotype. The text in our logo is transparent, just as we as a company are open and transparent when engaging with our stakeholders. 


# Exclusion Zones
The logo exclusion zone distance is a minimum one-height of the logo on all sides.
Usually, it looks better to have a lot more clear space around the logo.
