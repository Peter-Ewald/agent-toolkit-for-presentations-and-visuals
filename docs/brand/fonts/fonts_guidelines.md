PRIMARY TYPEFACE
NunitoCustom is our primary typeface. It is a modern sans-serif typeface designed for clarity. Its rounded terminals and balanced proportions create a humanist yet professional tone. As a Google Font, NunitoCustom ensures accessibility and consistency across platforms, supporting a wide range of weights for flexible design solutions and making it ideal for digital and print applications.
Use the following weights to maintain a clean, approachable aesthetic that aligns with our brand’s emphasis on simplicity and readability.

NunitoCustom Light + Italic
NunitoCustom Regular + Italic
NunitoCustom Bold + Italic


In MS Office apps (PowerPoint, Word), the Bold version will not be shown in the font list. To use NunitoCustom Bold, select NunitoCustom and apply bold in the top font control panel.
TYPE BASICS
Text styles define how typography is applied across all brand touchpoints. They ensure consistency, readability, and a clear visual hierarchy.

This section provides guidance on headline and body text usage, sizing standards, and examples to help you create content that feels cohesive and on-brand.

## Titles

Titles are the largest text style in our scale and are primarily used for covers or key opening slides. Their purpose is to create strong visual impact, set the tone for the content, and immediately capture attention. When writing a title we recommend to:

Use NunitoCustom Light
Write in sentence case
Left align the title
Keep it short and to the point.

## Headlines

When writing headlines, using different sizes can create a clear hierarchy, improve readability, and guide the reader’s eye so essential information stands out while supporting details remain unobtrusive. You can find below 2 different sizes of headlines.
Large headline

For key message, to create impact and immediately draw attention to the most important content.

NunitoCustom Regular
Size: 48 pt
Line spacing: 51 pt



Small headline

For subheadings or supporting labels. To organise information and maintain clarity without competing with larger elements.

NunitoCustom Regular
Size: 30 pt
Line spacing: 34,5 pt
